The Problem: Nutrition in Food Pantries

High calorie low nutrient foods contribute to increased risk for type two diabetes and obesity in clients. The obesity-poverty paradox describes that low income households eat more economical calories i.e. cheap high calorie, sugary foods. In the long term however this can lead to serious health complication.

Potluck: The Solution

Potluck is a website that allows pantries to communicate among themselves and distribute low calorie, high nutrient foods from areas of high concentration to pantries with less or no low calorie options.
